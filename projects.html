Ice Guy

About

Ice Guy is a game that 2 other programmers and I developed where you play as an Ice Cube guy that needs to avoid the sunlight so that he does not die. He must do this while platforming his way to the end of the level. This game was created in Game Maker with all custom sprites. This was my first game that Ideveloped with a group of other developers and one of the first games that I ever developed. However this current build is not the final build because I have been unable to reach those people, it does have enough there to give you the idea of what the game was ment to be.
