Experiment on AI

Experimental Use of AI - Unity

This is less of a game but more an experiement that I decided to mess with. As you can see in the video attached, there are green spots which is food. These green spots hold a positive weight which will attract the jellies to it. This is usedto represent food. Then when the jellies are killed, the create a slowly spreading area of negative weight(The red blobs). This is to represent the fear they have of the area a jelly was killed in. The jelly will compare weights in the surrounding area of the jellys position. If any of the areas around the jelly has a lower wieght, the jelly will move to it. It continues to check 3 times every second. You can download the game here: DOWNLOAD!
